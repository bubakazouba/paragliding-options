Cert credit: $350 applies toward full P2 tuition ($1,750 minimum)

Decision matrix:
• Want 1-day taste, real solo, money-applies-to-cert → Soboba ($350)
• Want 1-day taste but ok with tandem flight → SD Paragliding workshop ($400)
• Want to commit to a full beginner cert at iconic location → Torrey Pines P1 ($1,400)
• Want maximum solo-day-1 reps + LA-drivable → Soboba or Marshall (Never Land / FlyWithJordan)
• Want the storied "first-hour-solo" school → Eagle Paragliding (Santa Barbara, 2 hr drive)
• Want CHEAPEST 1-day intro period → Super Fly UT ($200) if u're ok flying out, or Penguin Bay Area ($250) if Bay Area-bound; otherwise Soboba ($350) is the cheapest in-state w/ confirmed solo day 1.
